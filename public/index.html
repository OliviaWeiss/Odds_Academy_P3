Betting Terms

What’s the Money Line?
Pick who wins the game
Minus (-): Favourite (safer pick, smaller reward).
Plus (+): Underdog (riskier pick, bigger reward).

Example:
Patriots -150 → Bet $150 to win $100.
Bills +130 → Bet $100 to win $130.
What’s the Spread?
A way to make the game “even.” You’re betting on the margin of victory
Favourite must win by more than the spread number.
Underdog can lose by less than the spread number (or win outright).

Example:
Patriots -3.5 → Must win by 4+ points.
Bills +3.5 → Can lose by 3 or fewer, or win.
What’s the Over/Under?
You’re not betting on who wins — just the combined score of both teams
Over: Predict the total will be higher.
Under: Predict the total will be lower.

Example:
Line is O/U 48.5
Final score 27–24 (total = 51) → Over wins.
Final score 21–20 (total = 41) → Under wins.
